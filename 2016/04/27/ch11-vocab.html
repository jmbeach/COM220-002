Ch11 Vocab

Apr 27, 2016

Term Definition
eros intimate or romantic love
storge familial, friendly love
ludus playful love (changes frequently)
pragma criteria for a partner
mania manic blend of ludic and eros
agape generous and selfless (blend of eros and storge)
commitment A decision to remain with a relationship. One of three dimensions of enduring romantic relationships, commitment has greater influence on relationship continuity than does love alone. Also refers to an advanced stage in the escalation of a romantic relationship.
committed romantic relationships A voluntary relationship between individuals who assume they will be primary and continuing parts of each other’s life. (These include three dimensions: intimacy, passion, and commitment).
environmental spoiling The process by which proximity breeds ill will, when we are forced to be around others whose values, attitudes, and lifestyles conflict with our own.
hooking up Engaging in some degree of sexual experience with no expectation of seeing a person again.
passion Intensely positive feelings and desires for another person. One of the three dimensions of enduring romantic relationships, this is based on the rewards of involvement and is not equivalent to commitment.
placemaking The process of creating a physical environment that is comfortable and reflects one’s values, experiences, and tastes. Physical environment is part of relational culture, which is the nucleus of intimacy.
pragma Pragmatic or practical love. One of the secondary styles of loving, this is a blend of storge and ludus.
navigation adjusting and working through problems with your partner
relational culture rules of the relationship
calling old version of dating in which the man goes to the woman’s house and sits and talks with her and her family
godly family family was microcosm of the larger society. moral instruction was more important than building child’s self-esteem
democratic family family which stemmed from work moving outside the home. Family became a protective shell
companionate family celebrate personality. Smaller family size. The child will lead a better life than the parents
