  • Ask permission before giving criticism

  • Describe behavior and its consequences by recounting

  • Preface with an affirming statement

  • When appropriate, suggest how the behavior can change

Example

  • You have a roommate named Jill who is not taking out the trash

    • Ask permission

      • "Hey Jill, you have a second to talk?"

    • Describe

      • "I feel frustrated when I walk in the house at the end of the day and see trash piled up when the bag is full"

    • Preface

      • "Now Jill, you are such a considerate roommate and I love having you as a roommate."

    • Suggest

      • "Can we come up with a system for taking out the trash?"

Another example:

  • Susy smokes hella weed 

  • Ask permission

    • "Hey Susy, you have a second?"

  • Describe

    • "I don't like the weed butter in our shared space." 

  • Preface

    • "You're a wonderful person Susy"

  • Change

    • "Could you get a minifridge? I'll help you buy it"

Another example
