Bids

  		 

Someone's  expression that requests a connection to another person (Ex: "Hey how  are you" they return the bid with "I'm good" or  "nothing")

 
 

Turn towards

  		 

A positive  response to a bid

 
 

Turn away

  		 

A negative  response to a bid
